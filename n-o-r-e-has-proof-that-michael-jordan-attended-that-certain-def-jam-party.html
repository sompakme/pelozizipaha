N.O.R.E. Has Proof That Michael Jordan Attended That Certain Def Jam Party

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Earlier this week, veteran rapper N.O.R.E. sat down for an in-depth interview on the Rap Radar podcast where he shared hilarious stories on rap’s past, including a scenario that involved sports legend Michael Jordan.

According to the LeFrak City native, Jordan attended a Def Jam Christmas holiday party hosted by Mariah Carey. Allegedly, Jordan was notified of Redman and Method Man’s request to meet him, but he instead uttered, “F**k rap,” something N.O.R.E. said he saw with his own two eyes.

But MJ’s reps caught wind of the revelation and stated that the six-time NBA champion “wasn’t at this party, doesn’t know N.O.R.E., and is a fan of ALL music.” However, N.O.R.E. has proof to put an end to the back and forth.

On Twitter, the “Nothin'” rapper grabbed a photo from Getty Images and pulled a “well actually” to show that No. 23 was indeed at that Def Jam party hosted by Mariah Carey. He was also pictured alongside Jay Z.
