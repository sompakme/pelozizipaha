Quinta Brunson Vs Issa Rae: Who Has A Higher Net Worth?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Issa Rae and Quinta Brunson are at the top of their respective games right now. As the playing field in Hollywood continues to level by the day, the two African-American women have taken the opportunities coming their way to become two of the most influential creators on television today.

Brunson was one of the main star attractions at the 2022 Primetime Emmy Awards, where she emerged victorious following her first ever nomination. A brief mishap between her and Jimmy Kimmel aside, it was a night of affirmation for all the work that she has put in to get to this point in her career.

Rae has been on the scene for a little while longer than Brunson, but she too, had a tough path to follow to arrive at the top. That’s not where the parallels end for the two stars either, as they are both well known as multi-talented comedians, writers, actresses and producers.

With their careers currently on the up and up, who between Brunson and Rae has managed to amass more wealth?

9 What Is Issa Rae Best Known For?

Issa Rae’s most distinguished work is Insecure, the popular comedy drama series that she created for HBO, while also writing and starring in it. The show enjoyed a five-season run on the network, before finally concluding in December 2021.

Rae has also starred in movies like The Hate U Give, The Lovebirds and The Photograph.

8 Quinta Brunson Writes And Stars In Abbott Elementary

Quinta Brunson is the creator of Abbott Elementary, a mockumentary sitcom that airs on ABC. The show had a very successful first season, crowned with two Emmy wins out of five nominations this year.

Brunson also writes and stars in the series, whose second season is set to premiere on September 21, 2022.

7 Has Issa Rae Won Any Major Awards?

While Issa Rae has been at the game for a significantly longer time, Quinta Brunson has already beat her to the punch in terms of winning one of the very major awards. Rae has, however, been nominated multiple times for Emmy and Golden Globe Awards, only without any success yet.

She did voice a character in the short animated film Hair Love, which won an Oscar in 2020.

6 Quinta Brunson And Issa Rae Both Started Out With Their Own Shows On Social Media

While they are both now dining at the highest table in the industry, Quinta Brunson and Issa Rae both had to single-handedly chart their own path there. Rae rose to fame with her Awkward Black Girl, a comedy web series she made and streamed on her YouTube channel between 2011 and 2013.

Brunson’s own series Girl Who Has Never Been on a Nice Date went viral on Instagram, and brought her to the consciousness of a greater audience.

5 Issa Rae And Quinta Brunson Were Both Named In The Time 100 List Of Most Influential People

The extent of Issa Rae and Quinta Brunson’s success at the moment was further demonstrated by their inclusion in the 2022 Time 100 list of Most Influential People. Brunson featured under the “artists” segment, while Rae was included as one of the “icons.”

MCU star Simu Liu, Ukraine President Volodomyr Zelensky, and Spanish tennis legend Rafael Nadal were among other names who made the list.

4 What Else Has Quinta Brunson Been Working On?

There is no doubt that Abbott Elementary is currently Quinta Brunson’s bread and butter. Still, she has managed to imprint her talents on a number of other projects.

Earlier this year, she voiced the character Ivy in the animated comedy series Cars on the Road for Disney+. She is also set to feature in Starz’s reboot of the sitcom Party Down.

3 Issa Rae Signed An Overall Deal With Warner Bros. Worth $40 Million

In March last year, Issa Rae and HBO cemented their partnership when she signed a $40 million overall deal with the network’s parent company, Warner Bros. Inc.

“It’s really exciting to have that flexibility and just deepen the relationship that we already have,” Rae told Variety after the announcement that she had inked the mouthwatering eight-figure film and TV deal.

2 What Is Quinta Brunson’s Net Worth In 2022?

As Quinta Brunson continues to establish herself among the Hollywood elite, most outlets estimate that her current net worth lies somewhere between $2 million and $5 million.

It is likely that this figure will become a lot more defined in the coming years, as her filmography – and correspondent earnings – become more easily trackable.

1 What Is Issa Rae’s Net Worth In 2022?

Issa Rae’s wealth would appear to be more quantifiable at this point in time, thanks to her longer tenure in the industry and the publicity of the deals she has landed over the years.

Celebrity Net Worth estimates that the actress has amassed riches totaling to $8 million, definitively more than those of Quinta Brunson.
