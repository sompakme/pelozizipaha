SNL Adds Miley Cyrus And 30 Minutes To SNL 40 Anniversary Special

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Saturday Night Live is going all out for its 40th anniversary episode, and we’re here for it.

Airing Sunday, Feb. 15 at 8p.m,SNL added Bradley Cooper, Miley Cyrus and Billy Crystal along with an extra 30 minutes to the show, which is already three and a half hours long, for celebrities paying tribute to the long running sketch-comedy.

But the fun doesn’t stop there.

Not only will fans see some of their favorite SNL alum, but NBC has gotten all new-age and launched the SNL40 app for folks to watch more than 800 episodes, learn cool SNL facts and more about your favorite SNLcharacters.
