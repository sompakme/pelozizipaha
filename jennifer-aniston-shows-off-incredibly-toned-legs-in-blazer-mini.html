Jennifer Aniston Shows Off Incredibly Toned Legs in Blazer Mini

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Back at it again with the blazer dress!

Jennifer Aniston wore the sexy garment in a black, asymmetrical version that made her toned legs look miles long while at the L.A. premiere of Mother’s Day on Wednesday, April 13. She paired the ensemble with Eva Fehren earrings, a delicate necklace, Jimmy Choo clutch and matching ankle-strap heels.

The Cake actress, 47, is the latest star to opt for the steamy take on the androgynous style. Just days earlier, Kylie Jenner rocked a similar, suit-coat–like dress — a blue, velvet mini that accented her tiny waistline at the Marie Claire Fresh Faces afterparty in L.A. on Monday, April 11. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 18, accessorized her bold frock with a patent leather pouch and silver strappy heels.

The previous month, Bella Hadid wore the style — this time a distressed, faux-burned, double-breasted Moschino design — at the L.A. Fashion Awards, topped off with silver baubles and black stilettos on March 20.

And her sister, Gigi, actually turned a tailored Versace jacket into a dress for a Vogue dinner during Paris Fashion Week just one week prior, on March 15. She added a statement black belt to accentuate her shape and paired the getup with chunky slate and gold sandals.
