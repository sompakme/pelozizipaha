Noah Gray-Cabey Biography

Noah Gray-Cabey is an American actor as well as a pianist. He is well known for his roles in the television series My Wife and Kids and Heroes. He was featured on the television shows Ripley’s Believe It or Not, 48 Hours, The Tonight Show, Good Morning America, and The Oprah Winfrey Show. Moreover, he was also starred in the CBS medical drama Code Black as Dr. Eliot Dixon.

Noah Gray-Cabey Music

Gray-Cabey has been playing in a number of venues throughout New England and also Washington at an age of 4 years old. He has traveled to Jamaica for his first tour with the New England Symphonic Ensemble. He has continued on to Australia in July 2001, and also at age of 5 years, he became the youngest soloist ever to play with an orchestra at the Sydney Opera House and the Queensland Conservatory as well as the International Convention in Brisbane.

Cabey has recorded a CD along with his family. They perform pieces by J. S. Bach, Haydn as well as Vivaldi, among others. Also, the CD contains recordings of him playing when he was 4 years old. Gray-Cabey’s parents began Action in Music (A.I.M.), a project intended to help kids develop their musical talents as well as give them opportunities to perform in other countries. The money raised from the concerts is donated to hospitals as well as orphanages in those countries.

Noah Gray-Cabey Acting | Grey’s Anatomy
