Where Is Naked And Afraid Castaways Filmed?

Naked and Afraid Castaways was filmed in the middle of the South Pacific ocean on a tropical island. The nine veterans are stranded in three different ecosystems.

The show has been shot in three distinct remote locations in the pacific ocean at the Bermuda Triangle edge. If the survivalist doesn’t adapt to the isolated area, they won’t survive long.

On a remote tropical island, nine survivalists are marooned without maps and tools. Without anything, they should sustain for 21 days.

They got dumped on a deserted island where civilization is miles away. In teams of three, the survivalist swam in different locations on the same island.

Each team will begin on a different part of the bug-infested island, but by day 21, will have to reach its northernmost point, where international shipping lanes present their only chance of signaling a boat for rescue.

South Shore 8 Degree North Of The Equator

Coming from the south, the team of Andrew, Heather, and Patrick will be the first to shore, where fierce saltwater crocodiles lie in wait.

Sticking up through 20 square miles of the pacific ocean, the island’s dense jungles creep with venomous coral snakes and pit vipers.

Extreme heat and humidity breed swarms of blood-sucking mosquitos, chitras, and sandflies, and a deadly coastline of shallow coral reefs prevents boats from landing on shore.

Eight Miles To The North

Eight miles to the north, the team of Bulent, Kerra, and Na’im are next to enter the challenge, where deadly sea snakes patrol the volcanic rock shoreline.

They have to deal with sharks, sandflies, and millennials.

Six Miles To The West

Approaching the island’s opposite coast, the final team, Candice, Rachel, and Justin, appear six miles to the west, where vicious bull sharks grow to be over ten feet long and speckled caiman hunt in the murky mangrove swamps.

Weighing up to 160 pounds, with razor-sharp tusks, territorial wild boars scour the island for food and fresh water.

Without maps, they swim to twisted mangrove swamps, sun-scorched beaches, and dense jungles.

On their journey ahead, they find an abandoned military jeep, a shipwreck, and a crashed airplane. From this scratch, the challengers discover a new solution to develop the tools.

Including location, everything will be harder, as the challengers have to survive with their navigational skills without a tool or weapon.

In the previous season, at least the challenger had a map. The former challenges shoot in Zambia, South Africa, and Argentina.

Naked and Afraid spin-off, Last Man Standing, was shot in Oribi Gorge, South Africa.
