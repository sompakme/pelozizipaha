Wes Studi Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

In 1947, Wesley Studi was born in Nofire Hollow, Oklahoma, to Maggie Studie, who worked as a housekeeper, and Andy Studie, who worked as a ranch hand and was of Cherokee ancestry. He is an actor, film producer, musician, and author who is best known for his performances in the films “Dances with Wolves,” “The Last of the Mohicans,” and “Geronimo: An American Legend.” He is also the author of the novel “Geronimo: An American Legend.”

So, how much money does Wes Studi have? According to sources, Studi has amassed a net worth of more over $1 million as of the end of the year 2016. His fortune has grown mostly as a result of his participation in the film and television industries throughout the course of a career that has lasted almost 30 years.
