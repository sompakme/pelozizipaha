Gwyneth Paltrow Regrets Not Stealing A $300,000 Purse From Set

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Highlights

  • Gwyneth Paltrow's biggest life regret involves a criminal activity she wishes she did - stealing a very expensive purse, the Birkin bag from The Royal Tenenbaums.
  • The Birkin bag is one of the most exclusive designer purses on the resale market and can cost upwards of $300,000, with some selling for millions.
  • Despite her regret, Gwyneth Paltrow's success as an actress has allowed her to amass a reported net worth of $200 million, making her more than capable of affording a Birkin bag.

Gwyneth Paltrow revealed one of her biggest life regrets, and fans will likely be surprised to know that it would've involved a criminal activity, had she gone through with it – stealing a very expensive purse, to be exact.

During a 73 Questions with Vogue video, which was shared on YouTube earlier this month, the actress had a quick answer when asked if there’s anything she wishes she would’ve taken from set over the years. “I’ve actually never stolen anything from set,” Gwyneth replied. “But I wish I had stolen the Birkin bag from The Royal Tenenbaums.”

“Is it too late?” the interviewer asks, to which Gwyneth replies, “I think it’s too late.”

The actress is offering a very exclusive opportunity to two special fans.

The Royal Tenenbaums is a comedy-drama from 2001, directed by Wes Anderson and written and starring Owen Wilson. It features a star-studded cast, including Ben Stiller, Gene Hackman, Luke Wilson, and of course, Gwyneth Paltrow, who clearly still marvels over the accessories used in the film.

The movie recounts the darkly comedic tale of the dysfunctional Tenenbaum family, whose estranged patriarch, Royal, pretends to be terminally ill in an attempt to reunite with his gifted but troubled children, leading to a series of confrontations and reconciliations.

For her part, Gwyneth portrayed Margot Tenenbaum, a brooding former playwright characterized by her distinctive style, including black kohl eyeliner, a long fur coat, and her short hair sleekly parted to the side and secured with a clip. But the character’s signature accessory is a brown Birkin bag – the one that Gwyneth revealed got away.

It's unclear how much the Birkin in The Royal Tenenbaums retails for, but considering the Hermes bag is one of the most exclusive designer purses on the resale market, it likely has a high price tag.

One report suggests the bag could easily cost upwards of $300,000, though some Birkins have sold for millions.

Though the Birkin is likely worth a small fortune, it probably pales in comparison to what Gwyneth made for the movie. Her salary for The Royal Tenenbaums has never been confirmed, but she reportedly took home $750,000 USD for her Oscar-winning performance in Shakespeare in Love (1998).

Considering The Royal Tenenbaums came out several years later – after Gwyneth’s Academy Award win – it’s likely the actress was able to secure an even higher pay for the film, considering she’d established herself as a successful actress until then.

Even more impressive, Gwyneth would later take home $10 million for her role in View from the Top, helping her to amass a reported net worth of $200 million. Needless to say, Gwyneth can afford to buy a Birkin bag if she so wants; she doesn’t have a reason to steal one from set!
