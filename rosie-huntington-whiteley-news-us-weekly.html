Rosie Huntington-Whiteley News - Us Weekly

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Name:Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

Hometown:Plymouth, UK

Birthday:April 18, 1987

About

British beauty Rosie Huntington-Whiteley was born on April 18, 1987. Once teased for her full lips, the future Victoria’s Secret stunner was voted Girl Most Likely to Become a Supermodel at the age of 13.

In 2003, while studying at Tavistock College, Huntington-Whiteley was discovered by Profile Model Management. Since then, she has appeared in advertisements for Levi’s, Burberry, Karen Millen, Agent Provocateur, Godiva and Miss Sixty.

In May 2010, Huntington-Whiteley was named Megan Fox‘s replacement in Transformers: Dark of the Moon. Despite having no previous acting experience, the model had impressed the film’s director, Michael Bay, when they worked together on a Victoria’s Secret commercial.

Huntington-Whiteley dated Tyrone Wood from August 2007 until October 2009. She briefly dated French actor Oliver Martinez, though their relationship fizzled after a few months. As of April 2010, the supermodel-turned-actress has been dating action star Jason Statham.
