Kanye West Has Blue Contacts, Matches Kim Kardashian at Met Gala

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Stealing the show! Kim Kardashian and a blue-eyed Kanye West arrived to the 2016 Met Gala red carpet in matching silver Balmain ensembles.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 35, showed off her recent weight loss after the birth of her 5-month-old son, Saint, in a sparkly form-fitting gown with a high slit. Her slicked-back hair and smoky eye makeup gave the look a futuristic touch, and she even added some extra bling with multi-finger rings and an ear crawler. Kardashian told E! News on the red carpet that she was going for a “bling, sexy robot” vibe.

On the other hand, The Life of Pablo rapper, 38, went way more casual for the high-fashion event. He wore a beaded white and gold jacket with a plain white T-shirt, ripped Fear of God Selvedge Denim Vintage Indigo Jeans (that retail for a cool $895!) and boots. His eyes were even more piercing with colored contacts to go with the Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology theme.

The Selfish author was up late getting ready for the big night. “Midnight Met Prep,” she captioned an Instagram with her whole team around her on early Monday morning

The famous couple always makes a splash on the Met Gala red carpet. Kardashian has redeemed herself with her on-point looks in recent years after she was highly criticized for her 2013 Met Gala red carpet dress, when the then-pregnant reality star wore a long floral-printed gown.
