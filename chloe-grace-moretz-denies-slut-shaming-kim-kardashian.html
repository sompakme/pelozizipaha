Chloe Grace Moretz Denies 'Slut Shaming' Kim Kardashian

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Standing her ground. Teen actress Chloë Grace Moretz took to Twitter late Monday, March 7, to deny that she was slut-shaming Kim Kardashian after criticizing the reality star’s (many) nude photos.

The spat began with the 30 Rock alum, 19, retweeting Bette Midler’s post about Kardashian’s nude selfie from Monday morning. “If Kim wants us to see a part of her we’ve never seen, she’s gonna have to swallow the camera,” Midler, 70, wrote in her post, which has since garnered 73,000-plus retweets.

Then, Moretz tweeted directly at Kardashian, 35.

@KimKardashian I truly hope you realize how important setting goals are for young women, teaching them we have so much more to offer than-

— Chloë Grace Moretz (@ChloeGMoretz) March 7, 2016

@KimKardashian – just our bodies.

— Chloë Grace Moretz (@ChloeGMoretz) March 7, 2016

Some fans questioned whether Moretz was shaming Kardashian for stripping on social media, which the Neighbors 2 star immediately shut down.

There’s a huge difference in respecting the platform that you’re given as a celebrity and “slut shaming” something I never have done and –

— Chloë Grace Moretz (@ChloeGMoretz) March 8, 2016

– would never do

— Chloë Grace Moretz (@ChloeGMoretz) March 8, 2016

However, Kardashian was less than enthused about Moretz’s posts, and even questioned the young star’s fame.

let’s all welcome @ChloeGMoretz to twitter, since no one knows who she is. your nylon cover is cute boo

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) March 8, 2016

Thank You!

You have successfully subscribed.

The If I Stay actress isn’t the only one to have felt Kardashian’s wrath late Monday. Midler and Piers Morgan received the brunt of Kardashian’s barrage of tweets, which ended with … yet another nude selfie.
