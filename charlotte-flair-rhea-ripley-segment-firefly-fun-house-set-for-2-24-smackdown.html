Charlotte Flair &amp; Rhea Ripley Segment, Firefly Fun House Set For 2/24 SmackDown

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 2, 2024

Next week’s episode of is set to be a big one, as it will not only deal with the fallout of Elimination Chamber, but also set up one of the biggest matches of WrestleMania 39.

As confirmed during tonight’s episode of SmackDown, next week will see Rhea Ripley and Charlotte Flair step into the ring ahead of their highly anticipated match at WrestleMania 39. The two superstars have no love lost for one another, and it’s been no secret that Ripley has her eyes on Flair’s belt, but it’s unclear as to what will happen when the two meet in the ring.

Elsewhere, Rey Mysterio and Karrion Kross will face off in a singles match, and in a surprise announcement, Firefly Fun House will return, where we’ll likely learn even more about Wyatt and the mysterious Uncle Howdy. Speaking of Wyatt, he also sent a surprising message to Brock Lesnar and Bobby Lashley. Wyatt told the pair that whoever wins in their match at Elimination Chamber should “run,” potentially setting up a future bout between the two.

  • Rey Mysterio vs. Karrion Kross
  • Charlotte Flair and Rhea Ripley go face-to-face in the ring
  • A special episode of Firefly Fun House airs
