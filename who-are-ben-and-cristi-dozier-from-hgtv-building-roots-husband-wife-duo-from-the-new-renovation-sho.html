Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Ben and Cristi Dozier are American entrepreneurs known for establishing Root Design Company and 3AG Investments. Find all about their kids and net worth in the article below. 

Ben and Cristi Dozier established Root Design Company, a home renovation company, in 2004. They started their own company in Austin, Texas, where they grew up.

The Dozier family later migrated to Pagosa Springs, Colorado, where they still live in an old Victorian riverfront home. The couple’s company’s studio is located in a historic barn on the property.

Who Are Ben And Cristi Dozier From HGTV Building Roots? Their Wiki Explored

Ben and Cristi Dozier, a husband and wife team, have engaged in the home-decorating industry for several years.

The duo has accomplished some amazing projects for their clients, and they’re about to do it again on their first tv series, Building Roots.

In building roots, the husband-and-wife team will design, construct, and remodel boundary-pushing projects for the customer according to needs and wants.

The show will document their personal lives, involving their children and their home in Colorado and their work as home builders.

How Many Kids Does Ben And Cristi Dozier Have?

Ben and Cristi Dozier are blessed with four beautiful kids. Adelyn Elizabeth, Gunnison Alan, Benjamin Creede, and Christopher South.

For the couple, Colorado is a source of inspiration. They also find it to be a calm environment in which to establish a family.

Cristi says the family can go fishing, skiing, walking, or sitting around a campfire when the weather is nice.

The pair revealed with HGTV Insider that the company had a name before it was launched. “We also liked the word ‘root,’ which alluded to the source from which things grow.”

Ben And Cristi Dozier Net Worth Revealed

Ben and Cristi Dozier’s net worth is estimated between one million to five million dollars as of 2022.

The pair has collected a massive fortune as the founder of Root Design Company and co-founder of 3AG Investments.

They also own a coffee and cocktail bar and Root House Coffee + Shop. Cristi is in control of the bulk of the company’s management, while Ben serves as a barista at the café on occasion.

Ben And Cristi Dozier Career Details

Ben Dozier attended Western Colorado University after graduating from Westlake High School.

He pursued the recreation business and the service industry while also playing football from 1996 to 2000.

Ben and his wife, Cristi Dozier, established Root Design Company in Austin, Texas, in 2004, only to transfer the company to Colorado a few years later.

The pair started as a landscaping company and grew into one of Colorado’s leading property restoration, renovation, and rebuilding companies at the moment.
