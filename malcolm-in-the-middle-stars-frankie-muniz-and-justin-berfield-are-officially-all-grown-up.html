Malcolm In The Middle Stars Frankie Muniz And Justin Berfield Are Officially All Grown Up

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 20, 2024

The show was a massive hit in the early 2000s. What made 'Malcolm in the Middle' such a cult hit was the fact that so many fans could've related to those circumstances. The show thrived during its early seasons on FOX and it would last seven seasons total. To this day, fans are asking for a return and reboot of the show. In truth, Frankie Muniz likely wouldn't mind, especially given that he is forgetful pertaining to a lot that went on during his time on the show, "Truth is, I don’t remember much [of my time on Malcolm]” Muniz said. “It almost feels like it wasn’t me. Over the past 10 years, my mom will bring up things like trips we went on or big events and they are new stories to me,” he admitted. “I don’t know what the cause of it is. It’s not something I looked into, I just thought it was how my brain is — so I thought it was normal. I didn’t know I should remember going to the Emmys when I was younger."

The cast is clearly all grown up since then, Muniz is closer to 40 than 30, set to turn 36 at the end of the year believe it or not! Reese, aka Justin Berfield, is also 35-years-old. Given their recent posts on IG, it is evident, both are growing up and to a lot of people, are officially old!

Fatherhood

That's right, take a scroll down their recent IG posts and it'll become evident quickly, the two are at a different stage in life. Muniz is expecting a baby any day now, "I'm so lucky that my son is going to have the best mom in the history of moms. Any day now..."
