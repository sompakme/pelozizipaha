How Did Sarah Paulson And Holland Taylor Meet, And Will They Ever Get Married?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 26, 2024

Sarah Paulson shared a beautiful tribute to her girlfriend, Holland Taylor, praising the 79-year-old actress after she closed her show ANN, a play about the Governer of Texas Ann Richards. Fans of both stars loved the tribute, most of them saying it was incredibly romantic, and this isn't the first time the pair have shared romantic messages on social media.One of the most memorable exchanges between Paulson and Taylor was Holland Taylor's adorable tweet, where she shared a picture of Sarah Paulson with the caption "no one more beautiful."

Sarah Paulson's tribute to her partner of seven years had the internet gushing once more, unable to handle how beautifully written the caption to one of Paulson's Instagram posts was.

"The love of my life @hollandvtaylor is closing her show tonight. I have loved her for 7 years and counting… and tonight, as she brings this production in for its landing, I have never loved her more," Paulson wrote.

"She is an artist to the marrow… I happen to love a genius. That’s really the truth. The creative achievement of her life thus far was about a woman in pursuit of simple fairness… fundamental goodness, with grit for days. To be inspired by the artistry of the person you share your life with, is the most glorious thing in this world. I love you, I admire you, I worship you @hollandvtaylor"

How Did Sarah Paulson Meet Holland Taylor?

Holland Taylor is an Emmy-Award-winning actress, her most memorable role being in Two and a Half Men, where she played Charlie and Alan Harper's mother.

RELATED: How Does Sarah Paulson Really Feel About 'American Horror Story'?

Sarah Paulson is an actress who like Holland, has been in several movies and TV shows, but Paulson's most memorable role is American Horror Story. She also plays Mildred in Ratched, and fans are wondering what happened to the second season, which appears to be delayed.

The truth about the couple is that they met over a decade before they started dating, at a dinner party. The actresses didn't become a couple until 2015, after Holland slid into Sarah Paulson’s Twitter DMs.

The news of the relationship was first 'revealed' in an interview with WNYC. Holland said she was considering marriage to her younger female partner, but never mentioned Paulson's name.

A few weeks after the interview, rumors that Paulson was the female partner started to spread after Holland posted a photo of Sarah during a ballgown fitting, with the caption, "When captions fail..."

The couple has been smitten since, and have been together for seven years as of 2022. But there doesn't appear to be any marriage proposals on the horizon.

Will Sarah Paulson And Holland Taylor Ever Get Married?

Despite Holland saying that she was considering marriage back in 2015, it doesn't look like the couple will get married.

Sarah commented on the public's fixation on her love life, in an interview with New York Times back in 2016.

“My choices in romantic partners have not been conventional, and therefore the idea that it’s ‘other’ makes it compelling,” Paulson told NY Times.

"If my life choices had to be predicted based on what was expected from me from a community on either side, that’s going to make me feel really straitjacketed, and I don’t want to feel that. … What I can say absolutely is that I am in love, and that person happens to be Holland Taylor.”

The couple is used to fan and media obsession with their relationship, from fans gushing over their romantic posts to people speculating and voicing opinions on their 32-year age gap. Paulson has also spoken about the age gap she has with Holland.

“I think there’s a greater appreciation of time and what you have together and what’s important, and it can make the little things seem very small,” Paulson said.

“It puts kind of a sharp light mixed with a sort of diffused light on something. … I can’t say it any other way than there’s a poignancy to it, and a heightened sense of time and the value of time.”

It's clear the couple are extremely happy and seem made for one another, held by fans as LGBTQ icons in Hollywood, despite the age gap that has left some feeling a little uncomfortable.

But despite their age gap, the candid and beautiful way in which Paulson and Taylor respect one another and talk about their relationship makes them an inspiration to love openly and honestly. As Holland puts it perfectly in her 2015 interview with WCNY: "I am out, I live out."
