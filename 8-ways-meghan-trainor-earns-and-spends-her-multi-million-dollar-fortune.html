8 Ways Meghan Trainor Earns And Spends Her Multi-Million-Dollar Fortune

Meghan Trainor gained worldwide acclaim for her singing debut and now has amassed a $14 million fortune that she loves spending on purchasing family homes and helping non-profits.

Meghan Trainor was only six years old when she sang in the Methodist church, and through her family’s support and encouragement to pursue music, she began writing and producing songs. After writing, recording, producing, and releasing her first three tapes, Trainor became a songwriter for the band Big Yellow Dog. In 2013, Meghan Trainor recorded All About That Bass, which several recording studios rejected before being picked up by Epic Records, who released it a year later. The song became an instant hit and topped the Billboard Hot 100 charts, selling 11 million units shortly after its release. Trainor has released three studio albums since then and even won a Grammy Award for Best New Artist in 2016.

Through her music, shows, tours, and brand endorsements, Meghan Trainor is worth $14 million today. She has often spent her fortune buying real estate properties, planning cozy getaways, and celebrating extravagant events. Let’s look at how Meghan Trainor earns and spends her multi-million dollar fortune.

Earns: Music Career

In 2014, Meghan Trainor attained international acclaim with her hit song All About That Bass, which remained at the No.1 spot on Billboard Hot 100 for eight consecutive weeks, taking the top place in 58 countries simultaneously. Followed by its success, Trainor released her debut studio album, Title, in 2015, which clinched the No.1 position on the Billboard 200. The singer has collaborated with top artists such as Charlie Puth and John Legend and released two studio albums since then, Treat Myself and A Very Trainor Christmas.

Spends: Real Estate

After Meghan Trainor and Daryl Sabara began dating in 2014, the couple purchased their first house together two years later. The couple bagged a stunning Toluca Lake home for nearly $5 million. According to the Hollywood Reporter, the estate featured five bedrooms and 7.5 bathrooms. Additionally, Trainor owns another California property she bought for $1.7 million. In 2020, the singer and Sabara bought a stunning Encino home costing $6.6 million from hip-hop producer Doug Jordan.

Earns: Brand Endorsements

Along with increasing her fortune through music, Meghan Trainor has also been the face of many notable brands and actively uses her social media platform to promote paid partnerships. She advertised FullBeauty, an online brand providing clothing options for plus-sized people, and featured herself in a TV spot for the online game Bingo Blitz. Her biggest endorsement occurred in 2016 when she teamed up with Skechers for a series of promotions for their new line of sneakers. Through her Instagram, Trainor has endorsed brands such as Gerber and RMHC Global.

Spends: Family Parties

Meghan Trainor knows how to plan parties and has clear intentions on how she will celebrate the important events with her family and friends. For her wedding ceremony in 2018 with Daryl Sabara, the singer wanted to host an intimate backyard wedding and reception. With 100 guests in attendance, the event was lavish yet cozy for the family. Trainor wore a Berta bridal gown while walking down the aisle.

Earns: Shows And World Tours

While the singer gained substantial money from her record deal and studio albums, Trainor made most of her earnings from touring and shows. In 2016, she set out on her first world tour called Thank You, which grossed $6.1 million worldwide, as stated by The Things. Additionally, her two shows in New York at the Radio City Hall earned her more than $500,000. In 2021, Trainor also had an opening set for the popular headlining band Maroon 5.

Spends: Getaways And Vacations

After an intimate wedding ceremony in front of her family, Meghan Trainor and Daryl Sabara set sail on their honeymoon to Bora Bora Island. The couple also took their families on a tropical getaway to spend quality time in early 2019. Since becoming a mother in 2021 to son Riley, the singer is known to have trips with her ‘mom friends’ like Ashley Tisdale and Hilary Duff. As mentioned by Parade, the trio and their other friends were on a weekend retreat to the Rancho Valencia Resort & Spa in California.

Earns: Podcast And Reality Show

Podcasts have been an easy medium for celebrities to reach their fans, and Meghan Trainor has been actively sharing her opinions by discussing various topics with her brother Ryan on Workin’ On It. The podcast features a new topic every week with multiple guest stars who share their views on workplace culture, parenting, addiction, and the music industry. Trainor is also the host of the 2021 spin-off Top Chef series, Top Chef Family Style, which aired on the Bravo network.

Spends: Philanthropy

Meghan Trainor has always strived to help people and causes close to her heart. The singer did her bit to provide support through her charity at-home concert performances during COVID-19 and generous donations to hospitals and restaurants. In 2021, she appeared on The Ellen Show while dancer JoJo Siwa was the guest host and donated a whopping $100,000 to the Childhood Cancer Foundation to help children battling the disease, as noted by People.

Meghan Trainor has continued to expand her reach in Hollywood by working on her music career, becoming a host, and working on a groundbreaking podcast with her brother. The singer is currently releasing new singles from her upcoming album Takin’ It Back, which is slated to release on October 21, 2022.
