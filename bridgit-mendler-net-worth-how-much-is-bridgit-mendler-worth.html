Early Life and Education

Bridgit Mendler was born on December 18, 1992, in Washington, D.C. She grew up in Mill Valley, California, and developed an interest in acting at a young age. Mendler started appearing in plays and became the youngest person to perform in the San Francisco Fringe Festival.

She attended the University of Southern California, where she studied Medical Anthropology and Medieval Visual Culture. Mendler received an anthropology degree in 2016 and later pursued further education at MIT, earning a graduate degree in 2020. She is currently working towards a Ph.D. at the MIT Media Lab.

Mendler’s early life and education set the foundation for her diverse career. Her passion for acting and pursuit of higher education showcases her dedication to both her artistic and intellectual pursuits.
