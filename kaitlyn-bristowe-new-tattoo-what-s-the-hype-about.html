Kaitlyn Bristowe New Tattoo: Whats The Hype About?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 1, 2024

In tonight’s episode of “The Bachelorette,” Kaitlyn Bristowe is introduced to her potential suitors, and 27-year-old Ben Higgins immediately asks questions about Kaitlyn Bristowe new tattoo. 

The five-tattooed dance teacher from British Columbia lives and works in Vancouver.

She explains to him that they are a species of dove and that, like no other bird, they always return to their nest. 

Kaitlyn claims to have travelled extensively and even called Germany her home for a while.

She insists that she never fails to take a flight home and will never stop appreciating her roots.

Moreover, even after spending the night in the Fantasy Suite together, Kaitlyn claims Chris never once inquired about her body art.

Clearly, Ben is off to a strong start as Kaitlyn enjoyed Ben’s inquiry into her tattoos.

Growing up, Kaitlyn and her family—her parents, Leslie Hipkin and Mike Bristowe, and sister, Haley Jane Bristowe—lived in the small Albertan town of Leduc.

It’s interesting to note that she and her mother fear birds.

Kaitlyn Bristowe new tattoo: The motive and meaning

Kaitlyn has some ink, but it’s not birds. We know them as swallows.

The swallow is an ancient and revered bird. It represents respect, trust, love, and optimism. 

In the days gone by, it was a symbol of good fortune and unwavering loyalty for seafarers.

It symbolized the bonds of blood, friendship, and respect.

The swallow also represents a safe return.

Interestingly, when a sailor noticed a swallow, he knew that land was close, and he was almost home.

Most bird lovers would know this that Swallows cannot fly very far from land.

Once upon a time, sailors who flaunted a swallow tattoo demonstrated their superiority.

The swallows were a symbol of good luck and safety for the sailors.

Last but not least, it provided much-needed relief and served as a status symbol for those serving at sea.

Who is Kaitlyn Bristowe?

Since her debut on The Bachelor Season 19 with Chris Soules, Kaitlyn Bristowe has become a fan favourite.

Ultimately, the show’s men voted for Britt Nilsson to remain, but Kaitlyn was named the lead of Season 11 of The Bachelorette.

In addition, Kaitlyn co-hosted Seasons 17 and 18 of The Bachelorette with Tayshia Adams, which aired during Katie Thurston and Michelle Young’s respective runs as the lead contestants.

Kaitlyn has competed, been a leading lady, and hosted, but she has also had a podcast called Off the Vine, and her listeners are called Vinos.

What is Kaitlyn Bristowe new tattoo about?

Kaitlyn Bristowe has a new tattoo honouring her listeners and fans that she got inked on the inside of her upper arm: a bunch of wine glasses.

To brag about her new ink, she posted a photo of it to Instagram with the remark, “Vinos are with me forever!” See my new ‘Vinos’ tattoo by swiping the screen!

Kaitlyn explained the meaning of this tattoo by saying, “Yesterday was my 5-year podcast jubilee, and I PROMISE you, the community of ‘Vinos’ we have established means everything to me. My easy job is to bring people together and put a smile on their faces.’

In addition, she stated, “It’s what I was put on Earth to do.” And the Vino fans have been behind me from the beginning. 

They were perhaps the deciding factor in my victory and the resulting mirror ball. Viva las Vinos, amigos! Is there anyone else that wants to get one to match?

Kaitlyn’s excitement was evident in the shout-out to the tattoo artist @jonboytattoo.
