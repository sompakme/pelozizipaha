Emma Roberts Is Still Wearing Her Met Gala 2016 Hairstyle Two Days Later

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 27, 2024

That looks familiar! Emma Roberts loved her 2016 Met Gala hairstyle so much that she still hasn’t taken it out — two days after the NYC event.

The milkmaid-inspired braided updo survived a coast-to-coast flight and two nights of sleep.

Roberts, 25, arrived back in L.A. on Tuesday, May 3, in the same Dutch plaits, paired with an Ann Taylor top, Mother jeans and burgundy flats. The very next day it was clear she wasn’t done with the look.

The American Horror Story actress’ woven locks topped off a sweet sundress outfit as she ran errands. She wore a salmon hued knee-length frock with black Isabel Marant booties and completed the ensemble with a gray peacoat.

Roberts debuted the hairdo at the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Costume Institute Gala for the Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology theme. At the star-studded event, the Unfabulous actress donned a lavender and eggplant Tory Burch creation with Giuseppe Zanotti Design sandals.

The Nickelodeon alum might be taking a page out of Kim Kardashian‘s book by getting as much wear out of her hair as possible. Just after she gave birth to baby number two in December, Kardashian, 35, vowed to wear braids for “months” to “let it grow” and to avoid “putting heat on it.”

Should Emma keep her updo for a while?
