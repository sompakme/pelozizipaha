Nick Gordon Could Be Charged With Bobbi Kristina's Murder

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 14, 2024

UPDATE: According to TMZ, Bobbi Kristina’s autopsy has been completed. The Fulton County Medical Examiner reportedly stated that the autopsy shows “no obvious underlying cause of death,” and “no significant injuries were noted.” There will not be a cause of death reported until the medical examiner reviews the record.

After the loss of Whitney Houston‘s only daughter, the microscope is now hovering over Nick Gordon. The longtime boyfriend of the late Bobbi Kristina Brown will soon be under investigation by the Georgia Bureau of Investigation for alleged physical and mental abuse towards her. Her best friend, Bess Beckman, will be working with the GBI to investigate the allegations.

It was reported that Gordon allegedly lost his temper and assaulted her on several occasions when Kristina informed him that she planned on going to Los Angeles to further pursue her singing and acting career. Earlier this year, she tweeted:

Let’s start this career up&&moving OUT to TO YOU ALLLL quick shall we !?!???! !!!!!!!

There WILL be justice for my sister, if the judicial system doesn’t take care of it, karma will. #justiceforkrissi pic.twitter.com/EGrZtPqbro — Bess Beckmann (@bessmakesmusic) June 26, 2015

According to The inquisitr, the GBI may have been waiting on Kristina to pass away in order to officially make a move against Gordon. There will be an autopsy performed soon to determine her cause of death.

But then here comes the twist in this shady situation. Gordon is already planning his defense against his $10 Million dollar lawsuit as he hires Jose Baez, the lawyer who won infamous the Casey Anthony child abuse trial of 2011. In the lawsuit, Bobbi Kristina’s conservator Bedelia Hargrove claims that he not only physically abused her, but apparently stole over $11,000 dollars out of her bank account while she was in her medically induced coma.
