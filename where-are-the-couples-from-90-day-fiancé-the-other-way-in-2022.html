Where Are The Couples From '90 Day Fianc: The Other Way' In 2022?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

The stars on 90 Day Fiancé really go through some tough obstacles while on the show. It is no wonder fans love watching the show to see which couples can make it and which ones can not. Many celebrities are fans of the series including Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner, who began watching the show while stuck in quarantine due to the Covid-19 Pandemic. In many dating shows, it is always wondered whether the couples we are rooting for will stay together or not. Today we are breaking down where the couples from 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way are and if they are still together today in 2022.

12 Are Brittany And Yazan Still Together In 2022?

The relationship between Brittany and Yazan brought lots of drama in their story line. The pair clashed over their language barriers and cultural differences. This led to the pair needing to make a life-or-death decision on whether a marriage would work between them or not. Despite everything, Brittany and Yazan did end up splitting and is one of the messiest in franchise history.

11 Are Ellie And Victor Still Together In 2022?

Despite their fights and clashing out future plans on the show, Ellie and Victor are still together. Ellie has moved from Seattle to Providencia (an island located in the Caribbean Sea) permanently to be with Victor.

10 Are Tim And Melyza Still Together In 2022?

Tim and Melyza’s had a very rocky relationship when they joined the cast 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way in season 2. Tim admitted to cheating on her with his coworker and was trying to prove his commitment to Melyza. Everything worked out in the end as the couple did end up getting engaged.

9 Are Ariela And Biniyam Still Together In 2022?

Ariela and Biniyam were expecting their first child together when they made their debut onto The Other Way in season 2. They faced a lot of stress trying to adjust to their life in Ethiopia since moving from New Jersey. The couple were also working on getting their living arrangements ready in time to welcome their son Aviel. The couple is now happily married and are raising their son together.

8 Are Steven And Alina Still Together In 2022?

The couple may have experienced lots of ups and downs in their relationship, but eventually ended in an engagement. The couple is still together and recently did get married despite viewers hoping to see them break up. To this day, Alina and Steven are the most toxic relationship to come out of the show.

7 Are Deavan And Jihoon Still Together In 2022?

After returning for a second season of The Other Way in June 2020 Jihoon revealed he and Deavan have split up for good in August 2020. Deavan has since moved on and recently got engaged to her new man. Deavan and Jihoon are in a nasty battle trying to get custody of their son Taeyang.

6 Are Kenneth And Armando Still Together In 2022?

Kenny and Armando became the franchise’s second same-sex couple and showcased a very sweet and emotional love story on The Other Way in season 2. The couple moved to Mexico to live together and got married in May 2021 and are still happy as ever.

5 Are Tiffany And Ronald Still Together In 2022?

Tiffany and Ronald while technically still married on paper are no longer together and have separated but remain on good terms. According to In Touch, Ronald has filed for a divorce from Tiffany since making his relationship with his new girlfriend Instagram official in November 2021.

4 Are Laura And Aladin Still Together In 2022

Laura and Aladin were already struggling with issues within their marriage by the time they returned for The Other Way season 1 tell-all. The couple have broken up in 2019 and Laura confirmed in October 2020 that her divorce from Aladin has been finalized.

3 Are Corey And Evelin Still Together In 2022?

Evelin and Corey are happily married even though there are rumours speculating they have spilt up. The pair is certainly not perfect, but they are commited to one another and working through their issues to remain together as a couple.

Related: '90 Day Fiancé': All The Red Flags In Corey And Evelin's Relationship

2 Are Karine And Paul Still Together In 2022?

Karine and Paul welcomed their son Pierre in March 2019 and welcomed their second child Ethan in February 2021. However, in July 2020, the pair did hit a rough patch after a blowout fight. The fight resulted in Karine obtaining a restraining order against Paul for abuse and neglect. Pierre and Ethan both live with Karine, and it does not look like the pair will ever be getting back together.

1 Are Jenny And Sumit Still Together In 2022?

Jenny and Sumit were the most dramatic and intense couple on The Other Way season 1 considering Jenny had found out Sumit was married to another woman. Sumit's divorce with the other woman did get finalized, but he faced cold feet when deciding whether to marry Jenny. After nearly 9 years together Sumit and Jenny have overcome all the obstacles faced and did eventually get married. The couple is still together and are happily living together in India.
