Fernando Verdasco Carmona is a Spanish professional tennis player.

His career-high singles ranking is world No. 7, achieved in April 2009.

Verdasco’s best performance in a Grand Slam was making the semifinals of the 2009 Australian Open, where he lost to compatriot and eventual champion Rafael Nadal in five sets.

Fernando Verdasco is married to Ana Boyer Preysler,  a Spanish socialite and social media influencer.

The couple tied the knot on December 7, 2017, on the private island of Mustique in Caribbean.

Verdasco and his wife Ana Boyer have two children, Miguel and Mateo.

Miguel Verdasco Boyer

Mateo Verdasco Boyer

Mateo Verdasco Boyer is the second son of Ana Boyer and Fernando.
