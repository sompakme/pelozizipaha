Judd Hirsch Parents: Who Are Judd Hirschs Parents?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Judd Hirsch’s parents are Sally Kitzis and Joseph Sidney Hirsch. Joseph worked as an electrician.

Judd Hirsch’s father Joseph was born in New York, to a German Jewish father, Benjamin Hirsch, and an English-born mother, Rosa Hirsch Benjamin whose family were Dutch Jews. Sally Hirsch was born in Russia to a Jewish family.

There is no readily available information regarding Judd Hirsch’s parents’ height, body measurements, educational background, academic qualifications, siblings and childhood.

In early 2020, Judd Hirsch appeared as the historic Simon Wiesenthal, in season 1 of the Amazon Prime Video show, Hunters.
