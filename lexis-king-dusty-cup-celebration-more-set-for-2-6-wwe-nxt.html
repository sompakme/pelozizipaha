Lexis King, Dusty Cup Celebration, More Set For 2/6 WWE NXT

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 30, 2024

WWE is loading up the card for the February 6 episode of WWE NXT.

Two matches have been announced for the show in the aftermath of NXT Vengeance Day. Riley Osborne will take on Lexis King following a backstage brawl between the two at NXT Vengeance Day. In tag team action, Axiom and Nathan Frazer will face Edris Enofe and Malik Blade, as they had a confrontation backstage as well.

Additionally, Baron Corbin and Bron Breakker will celebrate winning the 2024 Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic. They beat Trick Williams and Carmelo Hayes in the finals at NXT Vengeance Day.
