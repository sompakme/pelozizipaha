How Tall Is CrossFit Athlete Noah Ohlsen? Height In Feet

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 16, 2024

Noah Olsen is a Crossfit athlete striving to become the fittest man on Earth.

He stands at 5 feet and 7 inches tall which is below the average for American men. However, his short height has not stooped him in his CrossFit dreams as the man has made it very clear that he aims to be one of, if not the fittest man on Earth, that too entirely through CrossFit.

He has become a mainstay in the CrossFit community for over a decade and has racked up many awards and recognitions since he made his debut in 2014.

Now, at 30 years old, he is still trucking along and hasn’t let his age or height stop him from achieving his goals.

How Tall Is Noah Ohlsen? Height In Feet

Noah Ohlsen stands at 5 feet and 7 inches tall when it comes to his height.

The average height for a man in America is five feet and nine inches, and Ohlsen stands two inches shorter than that.

As for men in Crossfit, their average height is even larger, as they stand over six feet in most cases. The traditional or stereotypical look of an athlete is always someone who stands over six feet.

He has always stood against the grain, and since his first season, has gone above and beyond his height goal to the point that his height is the last thing people comment about him when talking about his stats and awards.

Noah Ohlsen Biography

Born May 30, 1990, Noah Ohlsen is a 32-year-old CrossFit athlete and one of the most successful Crossfit trainers there.

He rose to fame after winning the 2016 CrossFit Games Open and has since passed to amass a large following on social media. He has amassed over 832K followers on his Instagram page and over 80K subscribers on his YouTube page.

His Instagram page is where he showcases most of his workouts to his fans and even teaches them.

On the other hand, his YouTube channel is filled with him teaching various CrossFit methods but with the added caveats of showing long-form displays of his Crossfit prowess, such as recaps of all the competitions he has been on.

Noah Ohlsen Is A CrossFit Athlete

Noah Ohlsen’s professional career is about to end, as 2023 will be his last CrossFit season.

Already 32 and now nearing 33, he has decided to end his professional career.

However, that does not mean he will be quitting CrossFit, as he will still do the sport but not professionally. Regardless, Ohlsen is leaving with quite the achievements as he has amassed a lot of trophies.

He started his career in 2011 when he was ranked 1320th in the world when it come to the Men’s division. He slowly and steadily climbed his way up. Moving from 1320th to 188th to 56th to 20th to 16th, finally reaching the 1st position in 2016.

He retained the top five positions the following year by coming in second but slid down to 25th and then 60th in 2017 and 2018.

He rushed back up to the top 10s in 2020 and 2021 when he maintained the 6th position both years but fell to 107th in 2022 and, perhaps because of that, has chosen to retire.
