Pete Wentz News - Us Weekly

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 22, 2024

Name:Pete Wentz

Hometown:Wilmette, IL

Birthday:June 5, 1979

About

Pete Wentz is a rocker/fashion designer who is the bassist for pop-punk band Fall Out Boy.

Born in 1979 in Wilmette, Ill., Wentz dropped out of DePaul University one month shy of graduation to focus on his music career.

In 2005, his band Fall Out Boy made it big when their sophomore album Under the Cork Tree debuted at No. 1 on Billboard’s albums chart. Five years later, Wentz started a new musical project, Black Cards.

In addition to his musical talents, Wentz also has a clothing line called Clandestine Industries.

In February 2011, Wentz’s wife of three years, singer Ashlee Simpson, filed for divorce, citing “irreconcilable differences.” The pair share a son, Bronx Mowgli. Now dating Meaghan Camper, Wentz will soon be a father for the second time.
