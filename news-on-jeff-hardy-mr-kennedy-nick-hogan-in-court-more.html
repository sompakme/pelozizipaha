News On Jeff Hardy, Mr. Kennedy, Nick Hogan In Court, More

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 28, 2024


Last night on RAW, Jeff Hardy and Mr. Kennedy each had new theme music. Jeff Hardy’s new theme is called “No More Words” and is performed by the band Endevetafter. Kennedy’s new theme is called “Turn Up The Trouble” and is performed the band Airbourne. These new themes can be heard on WWE’s upcoming music CD, “WWE The Music: Volume 8”. It hits stores on March 24th, the Tuesday before WrestleMania.

Hulk Hogan’s son Nick was in court today in Tampa, FL for a pre-trial hearing where the Judge reviewed his background information for prior arrests and convictions. While Hogan has never been arrested, he’s had a number of speeding citations and warnings before the August car wreck that left his friend John Graziano seriously injured. It’s possible this is the first step towards putting together a plea bargain deal that would prevent Nick from going to jail.

WWE.com has posted an update on Hornswoggle’s storyline injuries. According to the article, Hornswoggle is being evaluated at a local medical facility in the Anaheim area. They are playing it up by saying that he suffered head and internal injuries. The article also notes that he went into shock on the way to the hospital. WWE.com will “update his condition” as it becomes available.
