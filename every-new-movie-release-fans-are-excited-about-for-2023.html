Every New Movie Release Fans Are Excited About For 2023

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 18, 2024

A new year means a year that is chock-full of entertainment! Devoted fans and film buffs usually set their calendars and count down to the day of release of their anticipated films. Whether it be a theater release, or an exclusive streaming service release, it does not make a difference to fans; if they like it, they will go out of their way to watch it. Already at the start of the year, some trailers and teasers for the most hyped up films were released, which is adds to the anticipation even more.

After COVID and its consecutive onslaught of lock-downs, fans were really starved of great movie productions and the summer of blockbusters really took a hit. As cinema and film seem to be making a recovery this year, fans can't be more excited for all that is to come, and 2023 seems to have a lot of great bits of entertainment to offer. So without further ado, here are some of the most anticipated new film released scheduled to be released in 2023!

Related: Goth Is Back, Here Are 8 Essential Goth Aesthetic Films

8 Oppenheimer Marks Christopher Nolan's Anticipated Return To Form

Christopher Nolan has been absent from the theaters since 2020's much talked about Tenet. Nolan's masterful collection of creative endeavors or works he's directed are some of fans' top film picks of all time, not to mention a blast to watch. After Tenet's underwhelming release in 2020, due to COVID and limited releases, Nolan's most dedicated fans all rejoiced when Oppenheimer was announced. The film promises to deliver with an incredibly interesing story, the story of the man who was responsible for creating the first atom bomb, and goes all out on the cast. The film is starring Cilian Murphy, Robert Downey Jr, and Florence Pugh, and is due to hit cinemas worldwide on July 21st.

7 The Barbie Movie Is Sure To Turn Heads With Greta Gerwig's Social Commentary

When news broke out that Warner Bros. were cooking up a Barbie live action film, fans sighed in tandem "oh no." However, when it was revealed that the incredible Greta Gerwing was at the helm of this movie, fans' opinions and frowns turned upside down. Gerwing is more than a capable and creative director who always has a lot to say, and with Margot Robbie and Ryan Gosling as Barbie and Ken alongside many more comedic giants, news about the film just kept getting better and better. Barbie is to be released on July 21st too!

6 John Wick: Chapter 4 Is A Movie Fans Have Been Waiting Years For

The hype is REAL around the fourth installment in the John Wick franchise. Keanu Reeves took the world by storm when the first John Wick came out in back in 2014. The series is loved by fans, and they are expecting the newest sequel with the same energy that the films always give off. John Wick Chapter 4 will be released on March 22nd.

Related: Keanu Reeves Once Again Proves He's The Nicest Guy In Hollywood, Caught Helping The John Wick 4 Crew Move Their Equipment

5 Indiana Jones And The Dial Of Destiny Seems To Be The Revival The Iconic Series Needed

Harrison Ford is in yet another Indiana Jones movie, and fans are crossing their fingers after watching the last attempt at reviving the series was a major fail. However, Indiana Jones fans are optimistic this time with James Mangold on the director's chair with a strong all star cast. Fans can catch Indie at theaters on June 30th.

4 The Super Mario Bros. Movie Is A Make It Or Break It Video Game Film

Fans are betting that Chris Pratt as Mario will rework his Lego Movie magic in this animated film! As if the characters alone weren't enough to attract fans, the impressive cast includes Anya-Taylor Joy, Jack Black, Keegan Michael Key, and Charlie Day! It’s in theaters everywhere on April 7th.

3 Dune: Part Two Is The Continuation Of Denis Villeneuve's Epic Saga

When the first Dune film was released fans finally got to see the sci-fi movie Timothee Chalamet and Zendaya deserved. The visually stunning Denis Villeneuve directed sequel is expected to take the world by storm In November.

Related: From Austin Butler To Florence Pugh, Here’s How Star-Studded Dune: Part Two Is

2 There Is So Much Hype Around The Little Mermaid's Live Action Remake

Chloe Bailey as Ariel hopes to be part of fans' world in this live action remake of the classic tale. Though the trailer enticed many controversies, there's no denying that Bailey will definitely own the role. The film will splash its way to cinemas everywhere on May 26th.

1 Mission: Impossible – Dead Reckoning Part One Might Be The Craziest Mission Impossible Film Yet

Because Tom Cruise only believes in raising the bar in every single action film he's in, fans never seem to miss a release starring probably the world's best action star. From its name, fans could tell that this rendition of the franchise is the real deal, plot stunt wise. Tom Cruise and the crew will be back in theaters with a knockout on July 14th.
