American singer, actor and rapper Ice T has an estimated net worth of $35 million in 2013. Ice T is also a businessman, screenwriter, and record producer.

Known by his stage name Ice T, Tracy Lauren Marrow was born on February 16, 1958 in Newark, New Jersey, USA. In 1987, he was signed by Sire Records. Shortly after, his debut album titled Rhyme Pays was released. A year later, he launched the Rhyme Syndicate Records label which eventually led to the release of his sophomore album Power. This was followed by the launching and release of his other albums which include The Iceberg/Freedom of Speech...Just Watch What You Say, O.G. Original Gangster, Home Invasion, Ice-T VI: Return of the Real, The Seventh Deadly Sin and Gangsta Rap. His work on Back on the Block won him a Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group in 1991. In the same year, he co-founded Body Count, a heavy metal band which released its debut album a year later.

Currently, Ice T is married to Nicole "Coco Marie" Austin. He has two children from previous relationships named Ice Tracy Marrow and LeTesha.
