How to spell enough

What is the correct way to spell enough? Correct spelling for the English word “enough” is [ɪnˈʌf], [ɪnˈʌf], [ɪ_n_ˈʌ_f] (IPA phonetic alphabet). Is it enough or enought? Correct spelling, explanation: enough is the correct form, since the Old English form genoh ends in a similar way. Due to this origin the spelling remained with oh sound at the end. Enought is thus a misspelling. What enough means? 1 : in or to a degree or quantity that satisfies or that is sufficient or necessary for satisfaction : sufficiently.
