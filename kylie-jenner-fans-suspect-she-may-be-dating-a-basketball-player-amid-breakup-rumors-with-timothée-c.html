Kylie Jenner Fans Suspect She May Be Dating A Basketball Player Amid Breakup Rumors With Timothe C

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 5, 2024

Highlights

  • Kylie Jenner's rumored relationship with a college basketball player has been proven false after alleged comments.
  • Paxson Wojcik's joking remarks about Kylie Jenner sparked social media speculation about their relationship.
  • Recent rumors also suggest Kylie Jenner is copying Timothee Chalamet's ex-girlfriend, Lily-Rose Depp, leading to backlash.

Kylie Jenner has grown up in the spotlight, and millions of people are fascinated by her life. Everything Jenner does gets attention, so when she is romantically linked to someone, fans take notice. But the latest report about her dating a college basketball player has quickly been proven to be false — even if some people may have wanted it to be real.

According to TMZ, the basketball player is 23-year-old Paxson Wojcik. In a recent video that was taken of him, he appeared to suggest that "Kylie was interested in him in the digital world," the outlet reports. As for where the rumors started? It's believed that was Wojcik's fault.

What Has Been Said About Kylie Jenner And Paxson Wojcik Dating?

According to TMZ, in a post-game interview, a reporter asked Wojcik', "What do you have to say about rumors that Kylie slid into your DMs?" The outlet notes that Wojcik had previously made a post on social media about "them Kylie Jenner lips." When the reporter asked the shooting guard to comment on these "rumors," he replied by saying, "I can't speak on that either." It does appear that he was joking. Still, that has not stopped people from taking to social media to question what is going on.

He has also modeled for Kim Kardashian's brand, Skims. It was always incredibly unlikely that Wojcik and Jenner were a couple, but even his manager, TJ Beisner, commented on the situation in an interview with TMZ. He said Wojcik and Jenner had not spoken, and "he didn't mean any harm ... and he now realizes he was inappropriate with his joking." He has reportedly since relayed a message of regret to Jenner's team.

Jenner just can't catch a break, and she is currently dealing with breakup rumors about her relationship with Timothée Chalamet. There is no real evidence to suggest they are no longer together. Still, that hasn't stopped the chatter, and the most recent reports about Jenner now suggest she is copying Chalamet's ex-girlfriend, Lily-Rose Depp.

What Was Said About Kylie Jenner And Timothée Chalamet's Ex-Girlfriend?

Chalamet dated Lily-Rose Depp at the end of 2018, but their relationship was over a year later. Their coupling received a lot of attention, and when fans saw photos of them kissing on a yacht, everyone had an opinion. Chalamet addressed this in an interview with GQ in 2020.

"I went to bed that night thinking that was one of the best days of my life," he said. "I was on this boat all day with someone I really loved, and closing my eyes, I was like, indisputably, 'That was great.' And then waking up to all these pictures and feeling embarrassed and looking like a real nob? All pale? And then people are like: 'This is a PR stunt.' A PR stunt?! Do you think I'd want to look like that in front of all of you?!"
