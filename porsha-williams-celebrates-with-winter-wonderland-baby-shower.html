Porsha Williams Celebrates With Winter Wonderland Baby Shower

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Party time! Pregnant Porsha Williams and her fiancé, Dennis McKinley, geared up for their first child’s arrival with a winter wonderland baby shower.

The Real Housewives of Atlanta star, 37, threw the baby bash on February 24. She invited some of her Bravo costars, including Kenya Moore and Cynthia Bailey, who showed up in white to match the party’s white-and-rose-gold theme.

“Since Baby PJ is going to be my little princess, I wanted to focus on a winter wonderland theme right away,” she told Us Weekly exclusively. “Because my shower was in February, winter was for sure a theme I wanted to stick with, but I also wanted all the glamour! … It really ended up feeling like we were in the middle of a fairy tale.”

She added: “One of my favorite gifts was from my mother. She bought Baby PJ a giant teddy bear that I just know she will adore!”

The mom-to-be stunned in a blush dress accented with pink flowers and gold sparkles that hugged her baby bump. Her fiancé, 38, sported a sleek gray suit.

Us Weekly confirmed that she and McKinley are expecting their first child together in September 2018. “I am happy, healthy… and pregnant! #BabyMcKinley,” she captioned a baby bump photo on Instagram at the time. “Love you all thank you for your support and warm wishes it means the world to us!”

Later that same month, the reality star revealed that she was engaged by rocking a massive diamond ring in a social media upload. “Feed off of good energy or bad energy will feed on you!” she captioned the pic.
