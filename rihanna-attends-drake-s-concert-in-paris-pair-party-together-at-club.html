Rihanna Attends Drake's Concert in Paris, Pair Party Together at Club

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Platonic party pals? Rihanna and Drake have been spending time together in the ultra-romantic City of Lights this week. The 26-year-old "Diamonds" singer attended the 27-year-old "Started from the Bottom" rapper's concert at the Palais Omnisport de Bercy in Paris on Monday, Feb. 24.

An eyewitness tells Us Weekly that Rihanna was in the audience at the show. After, she was photographed meeting up with the performer at Club 77. "She didn't want to be seen at all," an onlooker tells Us. "She kept her head down and charged through to the back of the club with her friends."

"They had a private space and stayed there most of the time," the eyewitness adds. "Drake looked very tired, but was smiling around Rihanna. They chatted, danced a little, and seemed close."

That wasn't even their first hangout this week in Paris. The twosome were also spotted grabbing dinner together the night before. According to the Daily Mail, Rihanna and Drake left L'Avenue separately to hide from photographers. The "We Found Love" singer, wearing a long-sleeve brown shirt, and metallic pumps, covered her face with a fur scarf.
